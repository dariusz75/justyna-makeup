About me

I am professional make up artist based in London. Fully qualified professional makeup artist with a diploma from the highly acclaimed Academy of Freelance Makeup. Accomplished and creative in all aspect of fashion, editorial, weddings, events, and lifestyle make up. Beauty, art, fashion, and design have always been my interests as I pull inspiration from the best trends to bring fresh styles to my clients. My passion lies in creating unique and distinctive looks for my clients by emphasizing natural beauty in each individual face. Finding the perfect make up style for my clients, whether the event calls for simply natural or elaborately dramatic, it's always my priority. For me beauty is something that can always be found in each individual face. My style reflects this view because I don't try to change natural features, I just enhance them. I also recognize that each of my clients has an unique and personal style and the makeup should reflect that. I use only the highest quality products from MAC, Clarins, NARS, Bobbi Brown, Hourglass, Laura Mercier, Chanel, Tom Ford and other leading brands. You have the benefit of "cream of the crop," tried-and-true cosmetics used in your makeup application. The result is makeup that looks and feels beautiful for the longest wear possible. For inspiration be sure to take a look at my Portfolio.

I hope you enjoyed looking through my site, and I look forward to meeting you soon.

Justyna
